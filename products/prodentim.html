What Is ProDentim?

ProDentim is an oral probiotic supplement designed to restore a healthy balance of bacteria in the mouth, strengthen teeth and gums, and help with bad breath.

Instead of only “killing” bacteria like many toothpastes and mouthwashes, ProDentim adds 3.5 billion beneficial bacteria and nutrients that support the oral microbiome – the natural community of microorganisms in the mouth.

How Does ProDentim Work?

The idea behind ProDentim is that problems such as cavities, plaque, sensitive gums and bad breath often arise when harmful bacteria dominate in the mouth.

ProDentim delivers specific probiotic strains directly into the oral cavity through chewable tablets, so that good bacteria can colonize the mouth, crowd out harmful microbes and support healthier saliva, gums and teeth.

Key Ingredients Overview

Who Is ProDentim Best For?

ProDentim is intended for adults who want extra protection for teeth and gums, especially those who experience frequent cavities, sensitive gums or bad breath despite regular brushing.

It may also be interesting for people with a history of frequent antibiotics or aggressive mouthwashes that may have disrupted the balance of bacteria in the mouth.

How to Use ProDentim

According to the official instructions, ProDentim is taken as a chewable tablet once per day, which should be slowly chewed or allowed to dissolve in the mouth instead of being swallowed with water.

This method lets the probiotics act directly in the mouth before reaching the digestive tract, increasing the chance that they colonize the gums, tongue and tooth surfaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast does ProDentim work?

Some users report fresher breath and less buildup on teeth within a few weeks, while changes in gum health and sensitivity are usually more noticeable after longer, consistent use.

Do I still need to brush my teeth?

Yes – ProDentim is a supplement, not a replacement for hygiene. Brushing twice a day, flossing and regular dental visits are still recommended, with ProDentim as added support.

Can someone with existing dental problems use ProDentim?

ProDentim may support gums and teeth, but people with severe pain, infections or advanced periodontal disease should first visit a dentist and follow the treatment plan before relying on any supplement.
